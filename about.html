Our Story

Discover the journey behind Green Zone's commitment to bringing you the world's finest Ceylon cinnamon.

Our Mission

At Green Zone, our mission is to bring the authentic taste and health benefits of Ceylon cinnamon to the world while supporting sustainable farming practices and the communities that grow this precious spice.

We believe in preserving traditional methods of harvesting and processing cinnamon that have been passed down through generations, while implementing modern quality control standards to ensure consistently excellent products.

Our Journey

From a small family operation to a global supplier of premium cinnamon products.

2005

The Beginning

Green Zone was founded by the Perera family in the highland regions of Sri Lanka, with a vision to share authentic Ceylon cinnamon with the world.

2010

International Expansion

We began exporting our cinnamon products to Europe and North America, establishing relationships with gourmet food retailers and health food stores.

2015

Organic Certification

We achieved organic certification for all our products, formalizing our long-standing commitment to natural and sustainable farming practices.

2020

Digital Transformation

We launched our e-commerce platform to bring our products directly to consumers worldwide, while continuing to support our network of retail partners.
